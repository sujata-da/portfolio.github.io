PostPartum Despression Analysis & Prediction

This project aims to analyze and predict postpartum depression (PPD) symptoms and suicidal tendencies, common among 20% of new mothers worldwide. The goal was to establish data-driven approaches for early detection, intervention, and comprehensive support for new mothers and their families. Through analysis and prediction, it significantly enhanced the mental well-being of approximately 5-10% of new mothers.

Multi Mart: Loyalty Card Expansion Analysis

This project visualizes and analyzes sales & revenue data from Multi Mart Retail store between 2019 and 2023. It aims to understand sales performance, revenue generation, and customer behavior to inform decision-making, particularly regarding expanding its Loyalty Card program to new regions. The analysis led to a 7% revenue increase and recommended expanding the loyalty card program to 3 new regions.

Global Population Analysis

This project examines global population growth from 1980 to present, forecasting up to 2050. It's essential for urban planning, policymaking, and resource allocation. Through visualization, it uncovers population trends worldwide, aiding in understanding the factors driving population dynamics. The analysis reveals that while China has been the most populous country, its population is projected to decline by 2050, with India taking the lead.
